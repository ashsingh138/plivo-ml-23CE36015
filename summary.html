🚀 LLM Speedrun Summary

Goal: Optimize a 2M parameter GPT model in purely 2,000 steps to achieve the lowest Bits Per Byte (BPB) on a mixed English/Hindi corpus.

Final BPB Score: 1.8138
Final Model Params: 1,967,808
Tokens Evaluated: 66,161 (Down from 159,225)

🧠 Architecture & Modifications

  • Custom BPE Tokenizer: Replaced the 256-vocab byte tokenizer with a 768-vocab BPE trained on the fly. This solved the massive token bloat caused by Devanagari characters, compressing the evaluation tokens by nearly 60% and effectively doubling the context window.
  • Weight Tying & Width: Enabled tie_weights = True for the LM head and token embeddings. The saved parameters allowed expanding the embedding dimension (n_embd) to 192 while staying securely under the 2M cap.
  • Lean Architecture: Substituted LayerNorm for RMSNorm and removed bias=False in all Linear layers to increase training speed and efficiency.
  • Training Loop: Transitioned from standard Adam to AdamW (weight_decay=0.1), added gradient clipping (1.0), and utilized a Cosine Annealing LR Scheduler with a 200-step linear warmup.
  • CPU Speed Optimizations: Injected torch.set_num_threads(os.cpu_count()), removed gradient accumulation, and adjusted LR to 2e-3 to ensure the run completed well within the 120-minute physical time limit without sacrificing convergence.
